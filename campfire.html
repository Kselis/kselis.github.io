The Digital Campfire
Imagine a cozy space where ten complete strangers cross paths for eight weeks. No rigid agenda. No forced productivity. Just a serendipitous collision of minds, romanticizing life and sharing whatever beautiful, fleeting, or profound ideas keep us awake at night.
The Experiment
The Journey: We provide the space; you provide the spark. You and nine strangers will organically guide the conversation. A philosophy on love, a thought about childhood, a book recommendation, or a random dream. It is a low-pressure environment for genuine human connection.

The Poetic Finale: At the end of our eight weeks together, a prize pool of ₹5,000 awaits the group. But there is a twist. The group must decide how to distribute it. Will you bless the five people whose stories touched you most with ₹1000 each? Will you hold hands and split it evenly at ₹500? Or will you do something wildly unpredictable?

The Catch: Your final decision requires a 100% unanimous vote. It is a test of the bonds you have built. Will you navigate the social dynamics, or will a stalemate leave the pool untouched?

Join the Circle: We are looking for ten strangers from all walks of life. No prerequisites. No known faces.
