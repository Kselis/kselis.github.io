About the Library

The KSELIS E-Resource Library is built for the community, by the community. Currently in its pilot run, the library's content is managed by selected maintainers to ensure high-quality, structured learning materials.

As we grow, we plan to open public editing and resource contributions, monitored by our core team, to build an inclusive and expansive knowledge base. This is an experiment in making world-class education free and accessible to everyone.
